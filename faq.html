General Questions

Weather Dashboard is a comprehensive weather information platform that provides real-time weather data, forecasts, and weather statistics for cities around the world. We use the WeatherAPI service to deliver accurate and up-to-date weather information.

Yes! Weather Dashboard is completely free to use. You can search for any city, view current weather conditions, 7-day forecasts, and compare weather across multiple cities without any cost or registration required.

We source our weather data from WeatherAPI, which aggregates data from multiple reliable sources including weather stations, satellites, and meteorological services. The data is updated in real-time and is highly accurate, though weather predictions naturally have some degree of uncertainty, especially for longer-term forecasts.

Current weather data is updated every 10-15 minutes. Forecasts are updated multiple times throughout the day. You can see the last update time displayed under the current weather information, and use the refresh button to get the latest data.

Features & Functionality

Technical Questions

Weather Dashboard works on all modern browsers including:
  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari
  • Opera
We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

Yes! Weather Dashboard is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. The layout automatically adapts to your screen size for the best viewing experience.

If the "My Location" feature isn't working, check the following:
  • Ensure location services are enabled on your device
  • Grant location permission to your browser when prompted
  • Check if your browser supports geolocation (most modern browsers do)
  • Try using HTTPS instead of HTTP (some browsers require secure connection)
  • Disable any VPN or location-masking software temporarily

No. When you use the "My Location" feature, your coordinates are used only to fetch weather data and are not stored on our servers. Your location information is processed entirely in your browser and is never saved or shared.

Weather Dashboard requires an internet connection to fetch real-time weather data. However, recently viewed weather information may be temporarily cached by your browser and viewable briefly offline.

Account & Privacy

No account is required! Weather Dashboard is designed to be accessible to everyone without any registration. Simply visit the website and start checking weather conditions immediately.

Weather Dashboard collects minimal data:
  • Search queries (city names) - not linked to any personal information
  • Basic analytics (page views, browser type) - completely anonymous
  • Location data (only when you use "My Location" - processed locally and not stored)
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

We use minimal, essential cookies for basic functionality like remembering your last searched city and analytics. These cookies do not track personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work optimally.

Troubleshooting

Try these troubleshooting steps:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Click the refresh button to reload weather data
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions that might block requests
  • Wait a few minutes and try again (API might be temporarily unavailable)
If the problem persists, please contact us.

Autocomplete suggestions appear after typing at least 2 characters. If they still don't show:
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  • Check if any ad-blocker or privacy extension is interfering
  • Try typing the full city name and pressing Enter
  • Refresh the page and try again

While our data source (WeatherAPI) is generally very accurate, occasional discrepancies can occur. Please:
  • Click the refresh button to get the latest data
  • Verify you're viewing the correct city (check for similar city names)
  • Compare with other weather sources
  • If the issue persists, please report it to us with details
